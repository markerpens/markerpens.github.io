Project Overview.

What is MechWarrior 5: Clans?

MechWarrior 5: Clans is a single-player, narrative-driven PVE BattleMech simulator. Players follow the story of Clan Smoke Jaguar and their endeavors to reclaim their homelands from the Inner Sphere tyrants. Players will experience this through the eyes of Jayden the protagonist, as he leads his squadron of 5 'Mechs across a multitude of planets to bring glory to the Smoke Jaguar name.

"We were made for this."

Responsibilities.

Content Design

Designed and implemented content for 37 mission briefing sequences. Reported to lead design producers to ensure that mission briefings met quality assurance standards.

Narrative Implementation

Worked closely with lead narrative designers to implement and review over 3000 lines of dialogue for mission briefing sequences, cinematics, and in-game levels for over 20 playable and non-playable characters.

Audio / Quality Assurance

Aided in implementing and bug-fixing audio files for all in-game content. Ensured that audio files matched the character and dialogue line they were assigned to for quality assurance. Reported any audio anomalies to the lead audio director.

Art and Texture Generation

Cooperated with engineering and art teams to generate 74 diffuse and heightmap textures and 15 Points of Interest 3D assets to ensure that mission briefing content effectively communicated the details and objectives of a mission.

Reflection & Skills Gained

Working on my first-ever release was an extremely enjoyable and humbling experience, especially within the cross-disciplinary space. Due to my brimming passion to deliver the most polished project to the table, I worked tirelessly to meet deadlines, especially during weekends, to ensure that the goal was achieved. But don't fret! This was concocted with a healthy balance of hanging out with friends, family, and working out.

Through this experience, I was able to further hone my creative and communicative capabilities, as well as my efficiency and technical skills in working within the Unreal Engine 5 software. But the most important thing I gained was the revitalizing camaraderie between the teams. There's just something beautiful about people coming together and working industriously and fervently to achieve a common goal. This is an important lesson I will take with me in my journey along this career path.
